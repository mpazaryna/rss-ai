OpenAI has released a new series of reasoning models called OpenAI o1, designed to solve complex problems in science, coding, math, and more. These models have been trained to think through problems before responding, similar to how a person would. The first model in the series, o1-preview, has shown significant advancements in solving challenging tasks, scoring higher than previous models in various fields. Safety measures have also been implemented to ensure adherence to safety and alignment guidelines. OpenAI o1 may be particularly useful for those working on complex problems in various fields. A smaller and cost-effective version, OpenAI o1-mini, has also been released, specifically designed for coding tasks. Users can access these models through ChatGPT and the API, with plans for future updates to add more features for increased usability.
